Dr. Tristan Lue

New York General & Cosmetic Dentist
NYU College of Dentistry
To find purpose and meaning in life is a beautiful thing.
Dr. Lue, a New York dentist, is expertly trained in assessing and treating oral diseases that have progressed beyond normal repair. With restorative dentistry treatments from Dr. Lue you can restore and improve your overall dental health.
Dr. Tristan Lue lives his purpose everyday by doing what he loves most; caring after and treating patients in New York City, NY. He found his life’s purpose while studying dentistry at New York University, and furthers his purpose and education by attending numerous dental seminars every year.
Every patient is treated with the utmost care and respect in Dr. Lue’s office. Their concerns and questions are genuinely listened to, and treatment options are always thoroughly explained before they begin.
As a dentist who lives to serve his community, Dr. Lue volunteers regularly for the Chinese Dental Association and also for the Queens Dental Society. He also speaks at local New York City elementary schools to help educate the youth on the importance of oral health and wellness.
"Dentistry brings me joy and fulfillment. It brings meaning and purpose to life. Every day I am grateful to use my skills and serve my local community." - Dr. Tristan Lue
